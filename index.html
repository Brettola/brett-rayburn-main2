What is Tech For Teens?

Tech For Teens is about giving teenagers the resources and technology they need to learn code and work with others. a chance at learning code, working with others, and gaining a new experience. We want to benefit the valley by giving the youth a doorway to something better. We want the teens of Fresno to be open to more than school and graduation. This is a chance to experience a new pathway to find careers and or simply new interest for the young minds, that need to be unlocked, so that they will know their potential, want to succeed, and achieve more than the views of mediocre. Here at Tech For Teens, we are hoping with your help of donating, that creating this opportunity to bring the youth together, will make them see a better future themselves.
